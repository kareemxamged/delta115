# Capability Matrix: My Courses

## 1. Course Discovery & Searching
- **Live Search**: Users can instantly filter their enrolled payload by `Course Name` or `Course Code`.
- **Advanced Taxonomy**: Dropdown filtering enables segmentation by Department or specific Semesters.
- **Timeline Segmentation**: Tab-controlled data views seamlessly split payload between Current (Enrolled) and Past (Completed) courses.

## 2. Telemetry & Analytics
- **Global Overview**: Calculates total GPA, Credits Earned, and Completion Ratios anchored in a persistent, sticky sidebar.
- **Instance-Level Tracking**: Each embedded card features progress bars, Exam Completion Ratios, and Average Scores.
- **Timeline Integration**: Upcoming Schedule lists naturally highlight future events mapped to their specific course instance.

## 3. Seamless Deep-Linking
- Quick action navigators via the card footers immediately pipe students into deeply nested sub-routes (`Exams`, `Grades`, `Materials`) bypassing manual navigation trees.
